About Me

My name is Carrie Song. I am originally from Shanghai, China, and I currently live in Seattle, Washington.

I graduated from George Washington University with a Master of Accounting degree in 2015. After graduation, I worked in public accounting for a couple of years. After I left public accounting and moved into the IT industry as a Business Manager of an IT consulting firm, I discovered my interest in IT industry and started learning coding.
